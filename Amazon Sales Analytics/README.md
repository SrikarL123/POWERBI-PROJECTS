
📊 **Amazon Sales Analytics – Power BI Dashboard**

🔹 **Problem It Solves**
Tracking sales performance across thousands of orders, multiple product categories, and different states is overwhelming with raw data alone:
- **Scattered Data** – Sales, profit, and quantity data spread across years and regions is hard to interpret manually.
- **Missed Trends** – Daily sales fluctuations and seasonal spikes are invisible without time series visualization.
- **No Geographic View** – Understanding which states drive the most profit requires spatial analysis that spreadsheets can't provide.

This dashboard solves these by consolidating 3 years of Amazon sales data into a single, interactive visual summary.

🔹 **Key Insights from the Dashboard**
- **Sales KPIs** → Instantly view Total Orders (1,611), Sum of Quantity (12K), Sum of Profit (108.42K), and Sum of Sales (725.46K).
- **Time Series Trends** → The "Sum of Sales by Order Date" chart reveals daily sales patterns and spikes across 2011–2014, with a notable peak in 2014.
- **Category Breakdown** → The treemap shows Chairs and Phones as the top revenue-generating categories, with detailed sub-category values.
- **Geographic Profit Analysis** → "Sum of Profit by State" bar chart identifies California and Washington as the highest-profit states.
- **Sales Gauge** → A visual gauge shows current sales (725.46K) relative to the 1.45M target.

🔹 **Why It's Useful**
- **Sales Managers** can track performance trends and identify high-value product categories.
- **Regional Teams** can focus efforts on high-profit states and investigate underperforming regions.
- **Executives** can monitor overall revenue health at a glance.
- **Inventory Teams** can anticipate demand based on category and quantity trends.
